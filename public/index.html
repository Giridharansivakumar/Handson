SOME OF MY FAV

FOODS


CHAPATI

Chapati, also known as roti, rotli, safati, shabaati, phulka, chapo, poli, and roshi, is an unleavened flatbread originating from the Indian subcontinent and staple in India.

PIZZA

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients, which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

IDLI

Idli is a type of savoury rice cake, originating from the Indian subcontinent, popular as breakfast foods in Southern India.
